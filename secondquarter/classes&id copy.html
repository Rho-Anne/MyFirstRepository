Pond Narawit

Naravit Lertratkosum (Thai: ณราวิชญ์ เลิศรัตน์โกสุมภ์; born 1 February 2001), nicknamed Pond (Thai: ปอนด์) is a Thai actor, model and singer.Naravit began his career after winning the "Go On Girl & Guy Star Search" in 2020, which led to an exclusive contract with GMMTV.He gained widespread recognition for his role as Mork in the 2021 series Fish Upon the Sky (2021).In addition to his acting career, Naravit is also a member of the Thai boy group JASP.ER, formed by RISER MUSIC. The group debuted on December 21, 2024, with the digital single "Sadistic".
